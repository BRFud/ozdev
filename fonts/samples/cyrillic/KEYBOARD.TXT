The following is the cyrillic character set as implemented in the
vp_rus.txt font.

Remember that the <>[{]}\|~` characters are not on the Wizard keyboard.  They
can, however, be accessed in MemoEdit.  Go to the help screen (press MENU
when editing) in MemoEdit to see how to access these characters.

a  - a
be - b
ve - w
ge - g
de - d
ye - e
yo - <>
zhe - v
ze - z
i - i
i kratkoe - j
ka - k
el - l
em - m
en - n
o - o
pe - p
er - r
es - s
te - t
u - u
ef - f
kha - x
tse - c
che - h
sha - [{
scha - ]}
tverdiy znak - &
miagkiy znak - ^
e - \|
yu - ~`
ya - q
